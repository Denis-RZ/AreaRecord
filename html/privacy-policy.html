🔒Privacy Policy for Screen Area Recorder Pro

Last Updated: мая 29, 2025

🛡️Our Privacy Promise

Zero Data Collection, Maximum Privacy: This extension is designed to work completely offline on your device. We cannot see, access, or collect your screen recordings or personal data.

📋What This Policy Covers

This Privacy Policy describes how Screen Area Recorder Pro ("we," "our," or "the extension") handles your information when you use our browser extension for screen recording.

📊Data Collection Overview

Data Type Collected? Storage Location Purpose
Screen Recordings ❌Never Your Device Only Not accessible to us
Extension Settings ✅Locally Browser Storage Remember your preferences
Personal Information ❌Never Not Collected Not needed
Usage Analytics ❌Never Not Collected Privacy by design
Browsing History ❌Never Not Accessible Not needed

✅What We DO Collect (Locally Only)

✅
Extension Settings: Recording quality preferences, format choices, and UI configurations stored locally in your browser
✅
Google Drive Configuration: If you enable Google Drive integration, your Client ID and folder preferences are stored locally for convenience
✅
Usage Preferences: Grid settings, autoscroll preferences, backup settings stored locally on your device

❌What We DO NOT Collect

❌
Screen Recordings: All recordings are processed locally. We cannot see or access your recordings
❌
Personal Information: No names, emails, phone numbers, or personal identifiers collected
❌
Browsing History: We do not track, monitor, or store your browsing activity
❌
Usage Analytics: No telemetry, crash reports, or usage statistics collected
❌
Location Data: No GPS, IP geolocation, or location tracking

💾How Your Data is Handled

🏠100% Local Processing:

  • No Servers: We don't operate servers that collect or store user data
  • Browser Storage Only: Settings stored using Chrome's secure local storage APIs
  • Offline Functionality: Core features work completely offline
  • Your Control: You can clear all data by removing the extension

☁️Google Drive Integration (Optional):

  • Direct Connection: Authentication happens directly between your browser and Google
  • No Credential Access: We never see or store your Google login credentials
  • Direct Upload: Files upload directly from your device to your Google Drive
  • Always Optional: This feature can be disabled at any time
  • Google's Policies Apply: Subject to Google's Privacy Policy when used

🔧New Features Privacy

📁Backup Functionality:

  • Local Only: All backups are created on your local device
  • User Controlled: You choose when and where to create backups
  • No Cloud Storage: Backups don't automatically go to any cloud service

🗂️Folder Selection:

  • Local Preference: Folder choices stored locally in browser
  • File System API: Uses standard browser APIs for folder access
  • No Monitoring: We don't monitor your file system or folder contents

🌍Third-Party Services

Google Drive (Optional Only):

  • Explicit Choice: Only used if you explicitly enable Google Drive integration
  • Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • No Other Services: We don't integrate with any other external services
  • No Analytics: No Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or other tracking services

⚖️Your Rights and Control

🎛️Complete Control:

  • Enable/Disable: Turn any feature on or off at any time
  • Data Deletion: Clear all extension data by removing the extension
  • Save Location: Choose where your recordings are stored
  • Offline Use: Use the extension completely offline if desired
  • No Registration: No account creation or registration required
  • Transparency: Full source code transparency in extension store

⚠️Your Responsibilities:

Important: You are responsible for complying with local laws regarding screen recording and obtaining proper consent before recording others' content or screens.

👶Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since we don't collect personal information from anyone, this requirement is automatically satisfied.

🔐Security Measures

  • Local Processing: All data processing occurs locally on your device
  • No Network Transmission: Settings and recordings never transmitted to external servers
  • Secure APIs: Uses Chrome's secure storage APIs for settings
  • No Authentication: No login credentials stored or transmitted
  • Regular Updates: Security improvements delivered through extension updates
